About me
I’m Lehi Galabo, a fresh graduate in Information Systems with a strong foundation in technology, problem-solving, and customer support. I enjoy tackling challenges, refining processes, and learning something new with every project. Whether I’m troubleshooting systems, collaborating with a team, or designing solutions, I aim to make tasks simple, efficient, and user-friendly.

My learning was reinforced through hands-on experiences, including an internship and a capstone project that showcased my ability to design solutions and support users effectively. These experiences allowed me to develop skills in system development, troubleshooting, and user support, while also strengthening my communication and adaptability. I believe in continuous growth, staying curious, and contributing value in any environment I work in.
